Vale x 1 HOME

Models who have access to Powers may choose Powers from this list when making their lists. Powers have a 12” range unless stated otherwise. Power effects are active until the user’s next activation or until the user dies (whichever comes first). Healing and Summon effects do not go away like the other powers.


No stat value other than Hit Points may ever be lower than 1 after all modifiers. No stat other than Movement may ever be higher than 8 after all modifiers. Powers require line of sight to the target. A Power can only be taken once per model.
